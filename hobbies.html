My Hobbies

I enjoy Math, Music and Programming. Among sports, I'm a big fan of Badminton. Tick hobbies from the list below by clicking on them to know more about why I like them!

I've always been a big fan of Math. The sheer elegance and power of Math applied to a problem brings a smile to my face. I've dabbled in a bit of Oly Math, Projective Geometry, Complex Analysis and Combinatorics. I really enjoy Number Theory and Linear Algebra as well. I haven't yet found a topic in math that I don't like :)
Recently, I've seen the Math of Quantum Mechanics and that's now on my favorites list. It's a must-do for any Math enthusiast. Finally, on my current to-do list is Field Theory.

I've been surrounded by music since I was very young. I have been playing the piano since long, and it has been my cent per cent guaranteed stress-buster during my 11th and 12th (and now too, of course).
I also like listening to music, specially orchestra and pop. Peter Bence and 'The Piano Guys'(Jon Schmidt and Steve Nelson) are a few of my favorite artists.

I love algorithms. Especially simple, short and clever ones. For this reason I also like Competitive programming a lot.
Recently, I've looked at Quantum Algorithms, and they are all so ingenious! Grover Search, controlled-U construction from AXBXC=I are some cool algorithms off the top of my head.
Grover is so good, it's easily a contender for my #1 algorithm spot.

Among sports, badminton is my favorite. I've been playing badminton casually for a few years now, and it's a great source of both fun and exercise. I'm open for a game anytime!
I also like watching badminton and cricket.
